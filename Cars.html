'94 Lamborghini Diablo SE30 Jota

In 1994 Lamborghni produced 150 special edition Diablos to commemorate the automaker's 30th anniversay. But Lamborghni took things a step further, commissioning is Formula 1 racing division to develop a special run within the already limited SE30. This resulted in the now legendary "Jota" package - a suite of performance upgrades installed on just 28 of the SE30 Diablos. The car originally went for 255k and has been sold for over $760k in 2016. #94LD1 is currently trading on Rally Rd. for $115 a share
