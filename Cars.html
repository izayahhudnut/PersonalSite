'94 Lamborghini Diablo SE30 Jota

In 1994 Lamborghni produced 150 special edition Diablos to commemorate the automaker's 30th anniversay. But Lamborghni took things a step further, commissioning is Formula 1 racing division to develop a special run within the already limited SE30. This resulted in the now legendary "Jota" package - a suite of performance upgrades installed on just 28 of the SE30 Diablos. The car originally went for 255k and has been sold for over $760k in 2016. #94LD1 is currently trading on Rally Rd. for $115 a share

Current Asset Value (Rally Rd.): $575k

'80 Lamborghini Countach Turbor

The Countach is arguably the most iconic car to come out of the 1980s The Countach Turbo is a class above that. Only 2 were ever built by famed Swiss Lamborghni Distributer Max Bobnar. #80LC1 is thought to be the most iconic modified Countachs ever built, it has been featured in numerous magazines and even as a toy model. Thought to be lost forever the car was found in a Nevada garage before selling for just shy of 1M in 2015. You can currently buy shares of #80LC1 for almost a 60% discount to the sale in 2015 at $64 per share on Rally Rd.

Current Asset Value (Rally Rd.): $319k
